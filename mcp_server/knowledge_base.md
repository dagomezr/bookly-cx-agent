# Bookly Customer Support Policies

## Return Policy
Bookly accepts returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. Digital books and gift cards are non-returnable. To initiate a return, the customer must provide their order ID and the reason for the return. Bookly will issue a prepaid shipping label via email within 24 hours of the return request. Orders over $300 require a brief human review before the return is approved, typically completed within a few hours.

## Refund Policy
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after Bookly receives the returned item. The refund will be issued to the original payment method. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a Bookly error (e.g. wrong item sent, damaged in transit).

## Bookly Credits (Alternative to Refund)
As an alternative to a standard refund, Bookly offers store credits worth 100% of the original order value. For example, a $40 order would receive $44 in Bookly Credits. Credits never expire and can be applied to any future Bookly purchase. This is the recommended option for customers who plan to shop with Bookly again, as they receive more value than a standard refund.

## Exchange Policy
Bookly does not currently support direct item exchanges. If a customer wishes to exchange an item, they should initiate a return for the original order and place a new order for the desired item. The standard return policy applies.

## Order Cancellation Policy
Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of being placed, as long as they have not yet moved to the processing stage. To cancel, the customer must contact support immediately with their order ID. Once an order is in processing or has shipped, it cannot be cancelled and the customer must initiate a return after delivery.

## Damaged or Incorrect Items
If a customer receives a damaged, incorrect, or missing item, Bookly will cover the full return shipping cost and offer either a replacement or a full refund at no additional cost. For incorrect items, Bookly will prioritize sending the correct items with expedited shipping at no charge. Customers should contact support within 7 days of delivery and provide a photo of the issue along with their order ID.

## Shipping Policy
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Expedited shipping takes 1-2 business days and is available at checkout for an additional fee. Free standard shipping is available on all orders over $35. Bookly ships to all 50 US states and select international destinations.

## Password Reset
Customers can reset their password by visiting bookly.com/reset and entering their registered email address. A reset link will be sent within a few minutes. If the email is not received, customers should check their spam folder or contact support for manual assistance.

## Account Issues
If a customer is unable to access their account or believes their account has been compromised, they should contact support immediately. Bookly's team will verify identity and restore access within 24 hours.
